About Tamizhaga Vetri Kazhagam

Tamizhaga Vetri Kazhagam (TVK) is a regional political party in Tamil Nadu and Puducherry, founded by actor Vijay on February 2, 2024. The party is dedicated to promoting social justice, equality, and Tamil pride. TVK has rapidly grown with over 5 million members as of March 2024.

Our Leaders

  • President: Vijay
  • General Secretary: N. Anand
  • Treasurer: Venkataramanan

Our Ideology

TVK draws inspiration from prominent leaders like Periyar E.V. Ramasamy, B.R. Ambedkar, and K. Kamaraj. The party upholds the principles of:

  • Social Justice
  • Secularism
  • Egalitarianism
  • Dravidianism
  • Tamil Nationalism
